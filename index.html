Labour Law
New Labour Codes Mandate 48-Hour Full & Final Settlement
As of April 2026, the Code on Wages (COW) Rules are now in force. Employers must settle all pending salaries within 2 working days of an employee leaving. Workers can now file expedited complaints with the Labour Commissioner.

Karnataka Rent Act 2026: 10-Month Deposit Tradition Declared Illegal
The Karnataka Rent Amendment Act 2025 has capped residential security deposits at a maximum of 2 months' rent. Landlords withholding deposits for wear and tear now face stiff penalties through the new Kaveri 2.0 Rent Tribunals.

Criminal Law
SC 2026 Ruling: Mandatory 14-Day Window for Preliminary Inquiries
Under Section 173 of the BNSS, police cannot use preliminary inquiry to delay FIRs indefinitely. Officers must decide within 14 days. Citizens can appeal to the SP under Section 173(4) via digital e-FIR channels.

RBI Mandates 8% Interest Penalty for Delayed Pension Payouts
The RBI Disbursement Directions 2026 require banks to automatically compensate pensioners for delays. An interest rate of 8% per annum must be credited without the pensioner needing to file a request.
